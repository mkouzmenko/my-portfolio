FreezerPro Acquired by Brooks Automation

***Existing FreezerPro® customers will continue to receive RURO’s outstanding Support services through their existing Support Contracts. New FreezerPro® customers will continue to find information about FreezerPro® on RURO’s website until the transition to www.brooks.com is complete. Through the end of 2017, customers may contact either the RURO or Brooks Sales teams for assistance. To contact a Brooks Sales team, please visit www.brooks.com and fill out their contact form.***

FreezerPro® is a class-leading frozen sample management solution that is indispensable to any modern scientific or pharmaceutical laboratory. FreezerPro® is an intuitive, fast, reliable and secure Web-based application that has a visually appealing interface which enables users to know precisely where a frozen sample is located even before opening the freezer door.

The LIS System With Limitless Possibilities

LimitLIS® is a modern cloud-based laboratory information system designed to increase laboratory accuracy, efficiency, and productivity.

By not requiring installation, LimitLIS® enables clients, patients and laboratory staff to securely access their data anywhere and on any device, while removing IT staffing costs. LimitLIS® also sets itself apart from other LIS solutions with new and exciting features. The client has the option to have LimitLIS® deployed with a fully integrated Client or Patient Portal. The portal is adapted to represent your company and has designed charts and graphs ensure that your outward web appearance is modern and professional.

Learn More

Cloud Platform For Life Sciences

Limfinity® redefines delivery of enterprise applications. Using Limfinity® empowers your I.T. staff to create a solution based on your specific requirements and launch it in a matter of days, not months.

Managing your data is simple with Limfinity®! The customizability of Limfinity® allows you to restrict the formats in which data can be entered, ensuring data standardization. Processes are able to be linked to specific data entry fields in order to allow users to enter data at the exact moments needed. Permission controls can additionally be applied for each discrete data entry field allowing data to be seen and edited only by individuals with specific access to those fields.
